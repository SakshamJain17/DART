Disaster Procedures

Disaster Procedures for Institutions

  • Maintain comprehensive disaster preparedness plans.
  • Conduct regular training and drills for staff.
  • Coordinate with local disaster management agencies.

Disaster Procedures for Schools

  • Establish school-wide disaster preparedness plans.
  • Incorporate drills into regular school activities.
  • Educate students and teachers about disaster response.

Disaster Procedures for Families

  • Prepare emergency kits and communication plans.
  • Identify safe zones and evacuation routes.
  • Stay informed via alerts and warnings.
